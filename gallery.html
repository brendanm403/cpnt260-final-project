Local Pokemon

Wailmer

#320

Wailmer are a common sight in north eastern Hoenn. This pokemon can eat up to one ton of food per day.

Wailord

#321

Wailord are the largest pokemon in existence. Despite this fact they are elusive creatures.

Swinub

#220

Swinub is a fan favourite in the area. Children love to interact with and feed them.

Piloswine

#221

Piloswine are often seen sniffing around for food. Their powerful noses find food easily.

Jynx

#124

Jynx are said to bring good luck to those who mange to encounter one. They are only found here in Winter.

Spheal

#363

Spheal are commonly spotted rolling down slopes into the water. They are quite friendly pokemon!

Snover

#459

Snover are great at camouflage. Many vistors walk right past without noticing them.

Snorunt

#361

Snorunt are the most endangered species in the area. It hides to avoid its many predators.

Glalie

#362

Glalie is a very rare pokemon to see in the caverns. Its scares most who encounter it.
