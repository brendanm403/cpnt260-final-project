Hoenn's Beautiful Ice Caves

Located in the far north eastern corner of Hoenn lies the amazing untamed ice caverns. Only a short ferry ride north from Mossdeep City and you'll be immersed in the home that many ice and water type pokemon call home.

The rugged ice caverns are a dangerous inhospitable place for any life form that is not adapted for living in these conditions. However it's stunning landscapes are some of the most beautiful in all of Hoenn. There are many rare pokemon to see that have adapted to these conditions over millions of years and our expert guides will guide you on a completely unique wildlife experience!
