Beautiful Sights But Quite Cold!!!

March 14th 2023

Jane Doe

  • Sights:
  • Duration:
  • Tour Guide:

Overall:

This was my first time visiting Hoenn. As I was staying in Mossdeep city I took the opportunity to visit the nearby ice caverns! Once you book your tour they pick you up from the Mossdeep harbour. It takes about 20 minutes to reach the the ice caverns. Drinks and snacks were provided on the ferry ride! The tour guide was friendly and provided lots of information about the geography of the area and of course all the pokemon that live there! They do a brief safety demo and then your are let off the ferry to explore the caverns with the tour guide.

The caverns themselves were quite beautiful, the ice almost looks like crystal when the light hits it. Unfortunately I did not see the pokemon I wanted to see most while on the tour (spheal), but I did get to see many wailmer and swinub. It was quite cool to see an entire group of wailmer close to the shores of the caverns! Overall I felt it was a good experience but it was quite cold so I think maybe the duration of the time spent in the caverns could be slightly reduced. Make sure you come prepared for the weather!
